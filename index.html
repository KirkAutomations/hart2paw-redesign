What We Do

Pet Care Services

From daily walks to extended stays, we keep your pets safe, happy, and loved while you are away.

Overnights

Overnight care in your home or ours while you are away—includes feeding, potty breaks, playtime, and lots of love. Flexible scheduling for short or extended trips.

Walks

Fresh air, mental stimulation, and exercise for your pup. Available in 30 or 60 minute sessions tailored to your dog’s energy and needs.

Drop-Ins

Quick check-ins for potty breaks, meals, medication, or just some love. Perfect for pets who need attention without a full walk.

Daycare

Your pet spends the day at our home with playtime, rest, and supervision. A great option when you are at work or out for the day.

My Story

Meet Bailey Hart

Animals have always been a huge part of my life. I grew up surrounded by pets, and I am a proud dog mom to two amazing pups of my own—Winnie and Teddy, who are both rescues. I started pet sitting at just 12 years old, and over the years, I have come to realize something simple but true: every pet deserves love, patience, and attentive care.

The idea for Hart 2 Paw came from my own wedding day. I wanted my dogs there for the biggest day of my life, but finding someone trustworthy and reliable was incredibly stressful. That is when I realized I could be that person for others—someone who truly understands how much your pets mean to you and treats them like family.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Hart 2 Paw offer?
We provide pet sitting (in our home or yours), walks, drop-ins, event day services (like weddings or photoshoots), and drop-off and pick-up services. We can tailor services to fit your specific needs!
Do you only care for dogs and cats?
While we specialize in dog and cat care, we are experienced with all kinds of pets!
How far in advance should I book?
For weddings or special events, it is best to book 1–3 months in advance to ensure availability. For regular pet sitting, a week in advance is great—but last-minute requests are welcome if we have openings!
Are you insured and certified?
Yes! Hart 2 Paw is fully insured and CPR, first-aid and Fear Free certified. Your pet’s safety and well-being is our top priority!
Can my pet be part of the wedding ceremony?
Absolutely! This is one of our most requested services. We will make sure your pup is clean, calm, happy, and present for the special moment—and safely returned home when it is time.
Will my pet be around other animals?
Only if you request socialization. Bailey has 2 friendly dogs at home. Otherwise, one-on-one care and transport are provided, unless your pet is part of a multi-pet household.
Do you offer overnight stays?
Yes, we offer overnight pet sitting in your home or ours for pets that need a little extra care and comfort while you are away.
Where is Hart 2 Paw located?
Hart 2 Paw is based in Los Angeles and serves surrounding areas. Long-distance travel for events is also possible with advance notice.
How do I book?
You can contact us through the contact form below, by phone at (310) 800-1744, or via email at contact@hart2paw.com. We will schedule a consultation to get to know your pet and their needs before booking.
What are your rates?
Rates vary based on services, distance, and time needed. Please reach out for a custom quote tailored to your situation. Hart 2 Paw aims to keep pricing fair and transparent.
